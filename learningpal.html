LearningPal

March 2021 - August 2021

Product Design Intern

LearningPal is a Berkeley SkyDeck company and an AI-Based enterprise solution working to digitize handwritten documents in various languages and serve as a document management platform. I worked as a Product Design intern to rebrand their document management platform for their launch in the fall.

UX/UI of Dashboard and Vaccine Project

Overview

As a Product Design intern, I had the responsibility of redesigning the main dashboard that LearningPal would be selling to primarily document-heavy businesses such as construction companies or airlines. We were also approached by a UK-based clinical startup for a Vaccine Passport project for the upcoming Olympics.

Result

In my time at LearningPal, I kept consistent communication with the development team and walked through the user experience of the platform while making sure each feature was feasible and effective. Additionally, for the quick sprint of the vaccine passport, I produced Low-fidelity and Mid-fidelity mockups that were then presented to doctors on site at the Olympics.
